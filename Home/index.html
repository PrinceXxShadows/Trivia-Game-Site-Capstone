About Us

We are Group 8, a enthusiastic group of three coders with a passion for making things fun. Bearing that in mind, we drummed up this little project. Each developer made two quizes each comprising of popular pop culture television and cinema. We then turned those quizes into fun interactive games. The player not only scores points as they make correct answers, they can also compete with their friends on the leaderboard, which is stored in the local storage of the web application.Credit for this project goes as follows. .
